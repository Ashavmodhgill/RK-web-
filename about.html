Our Story

RK Begin with a simple dream: to create a place where food isn't just sustenance, but an experience. Founded by Two brothers Balwinder and Rajinder Sharma, Both Were passionate about making vibrant Indian Sweets and dishes From trheir our journey started in a small kitchen in 1989 with a family recipe passed down through generations We believe in the power of fresh, locally sourced ingredients and the magic that happens when they're combined with skill and heart.

Every plate served at RK is a testament to our commitment to quality and our desire to bring joy to your table. From our signature [Smosa chart ] to our seasonal specials, we invite you to savor the dedication in every bite.

Our Mission

To delight our guests with an unforgettable dining experience, combining innovative cuisine with warm, impeccable service in an inviting atmosphere. We strive to be a culinary landmark, celebrated for our creativity, consistency, and community spirit.

Our Values

  • Quality: Sourcing the finest ingredients and preparing them with precision.
  • Passion: Infusing every dish with love and dedication.
  • Hospitality: Creating a welcoming and memorable experience for every guest.
  • Innovation: Constantly exploring new flavors and culinary techniques.
  • Community: Supporting local producers and giving back to our neighborhood.
